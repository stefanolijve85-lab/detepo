# 14 — App Store Launch Strategy & ASO

## Positioning

> **Neon Runner** — the first-person endless runner that *feels* like a console FPS.
>
> Sprint, slide, and ignite jetpacks through a neon megacity. Compete on the global
> leaderboard. New season every 50 days.

Audience:
- Primary: 14–28, mobile-first, plays *Subway Surfers* and watches *FPS movement* TikToks.
- Secondary: 28–40, lapsed *Mirror's Edge* / *Temple Run* fans.

Keywords for ASO ranking (priority-ordered):

```
endless runner, fps, first person, parkour, jetpack, neon, cyberpunk,
runner, dodge, swipe, run game, race, action, leaderboard, free game,
arcade, 3d runner, parkour runner, futuristic
```

We do **not** keyword-stuff. The actual title + subtitle target two phrases:

- **Title**: `Neon Runner: FPS Endless Run` (under 30 chars)
- **Subtitle**: `First-person parkour & jetpack` (under 30 chars)

Promotional text (changeable without app review) used for events:

> Season 1 — *Neon Genesis* — live now. Earn the legendary Dragon Jetpack before May 30.

## App Preview videos

Three 15–25 s vertical previews:

1. **Speed sizzle** — montage of 4 biomes, ramp from 0→max speed, jetpack ignition,
   leaderboard climb. **15 s**.
2. **Jetpack focus** — first-person flight, sky lane, gold coin shower. **20 s**.
3. **Near-miss compilation** — slow-mo dodges, drones grazing camera, crash recovery.
   **15 s**.

All previews captured at 60 fps from real builds, post-graded to match screenshot
treatment. Music: bespoke 20-second cuts of in-game tracks, ducked behind UI sounds.

## Apple Search Ads

- **Discovery campaign** at low CPT to learn keywords.
- **Brand campaign** to defend on "neon runner".
- **Competitor campaign** carefully on "subway runner", "temple runner" with relevance ad
  content (no infringement).
- Target tCPI: $1.20 in Tier-1 markets (US/UK/CA/AU). LTV target $3.50 D90.

## Influencer & TikTok plan

- 50 mobile-gaming TikTok creators × 1 video each in launch month.
- Capture toolkit shipped with the game: in-app screen recorder + auto-cut "best moments"
  reel that can be exported as a 9:16 MP4 with Neon Runner watermark.
- TikTok-friendly first-frame moments: jetpack ignition, near-miss drone, sky-lane dive.
- Hashtag program: `#NeonRunnerClips` weekly contest with in-game prize.

## Featuring pitch

We pitch Apple ~6 weeks before launch with:

- A 60-second cinematic preview built specifically for the App Store front page.
- A list of platform features used (StoreKit 2, Game Center, Live Activities, Dynamic
  Island, ProMotion, Metal post-FX, Sign in with Apple, CloudKit, ATT).
- A localized launch in 12 languages day-one.
- A polished press kit at `presskit.neonrunner.app`.

## Legal

- Privacy policy + terms hosted at `neonrunner.app/legal`.
